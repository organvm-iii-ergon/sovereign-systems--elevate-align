Most people jump straight to optimization — supplements, protocols, biohacks. But if the baseline isn't regulated, optimization is noise on top of chaos.

Regulation means your nervous system isn't constantly in fight-or-flight, your sleep-wake cycle is consistent, and your body can actually absorb what you give it. You can't receive in survival mode. A calm body is an open field.
