## Your Answers

### 1. The Spiral — Alive & Breathing
Gentle floating motion. Pillars orbit slowly around the center like they're suspended in water. Feels organic and calming. Not static, not chaotic — just alive.

### 2. Colors — Teal / Ocean Tones
Keeping the visual language you already have across your sites — evolved and polished, not reinvented. Think: deep ocean teal, soft aqua highlights, clean whites, dark backgrounds where it feels right.

### 3. Water Landing Page — Headline + Hook, Then Video
When someone lands on stopdrinkingacid.com, they see:
1. A headline ("Water changed everything for me.")
2. A few grounding sentences
3. Then your documentary video — front and center

This builds anticipation instead of throwing the video at them cold.

### 4. Quiz — Stays in GHL
Your quiz lives in GoHighLevel where it already connects to your tagging system, follow-up workflows, and contact management. We embed it into the site so it looks native but all the backend stays exactly where it is. No need to rebuild what works.

### 5. Pillars — All 4 Visible Now
The spiral shows all 4 pillars from day one:
- Physical Sovereignty (water — fully built)
- Inner Sovereignty (coming soon)
- Identity Sovereignty (coming soon)
- Financial Sovereignty (links to eaucohub.com — placeholder)

"Coming soon" pages are clean and intentional, not broken. They show the vision.

### 6. Editing Content — Both
You'll be able to edit simple things yourself — headlines, branch page text, video links — by updating plain text files. For bigger structural changes (adding a new pillar, redesigning a page layout), you call us.

### 7. What You'll See First — Everything Together
We build the full skeleton with both the hub spiral and the water funnel visible. The water side will be more polished (because the content exists), and everything else gets clean placeholders. You see the whole system from day one.

## How It Works (Non-Technical)

**Your content lives in simple text files.** Each branch page (gut, fertility, autoimmune, etc.) is its own file. You can open it, change the words, save it, and the site updates. No code.

**The spiral is custom-built for you.** It's not a template or a plugin — it's made from scratch to match your vision. The 4 pillars float around a center point. Hover reveals details. Click navigates. Mobile-friendly.

**Your GHL backend stays exactly as-is.** Quiz forms, tagging, email workflows, contact management — none of that changes. The new site wraps around it.

**One codebase, three domains.** elevatealign.com, stopdrinkingacid.com, and eaucohub.com all run from the same project. Update once, it works everywhere.
