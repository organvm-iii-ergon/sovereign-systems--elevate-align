## How to Use This Document

Every health-related claim on the Sovereign Systems Spiral website maps to one or more entries in this canon. The document is organized into two parts: **sacred/philosophical primary texts** that ground the brand's symbolic framework (spiral, sovereignty, water-as-life), and **peer-reviewed biomedical research** that backs every health claim made on the site.

**Rhetorical framework (pathos / logos / ethos):**
- Branch pages lead with human experience (pathos), bridge to data (logos), and cite institutional authority (ethos).
- This document supplies the logos and ethos layers. Pathos lives in the copy itself.

**FTC compliance:** The Federal Trade Commission requires that health-related marketing claims be truthful, not misleading, and backed by competent and reliable scientific evidence. Use language like "studies suggest," "research indicates," "has been shown in clinical trials to" — never "cures," "treats," or "guarantees." The claim-to-source mapping in Part III makes compliance auditable.

**How entries are numbered:** Sacred/philosophical sources are prefixed `S-` (S-01 through S-35). Biomedical sources are prefixed `B-` (B-01 through B-78). The claim mapping table in Part III references these IDs.
