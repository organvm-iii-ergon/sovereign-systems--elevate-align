🧠🔋 What is the Piezoelectric Effect?


Simple version:
When you squeeze or hit certain crystals (like quartz), they generate electricity.

🧪 Example:

That steady vibration helps the watch keep perfect time.


Now reverse that:


If you press or squeeze a quartz crystal, it makes electricity — like a mini battery.
