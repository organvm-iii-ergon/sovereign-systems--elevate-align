Dopamine: Yang / Masculine

Oxytocin: Yin / Feminine


Function: Bonding, trust, connection, nurturing

Energetic Quality: Inward, receptive, cyclical

Associated Actions: Cuddling, emotional intimacy, caretaking, community-building

Feminine Parallel: The “gatherer” or mother archetype — focused on connection, safety, and emotional regulation.



They work best in balance:


Too much dopamine without oxytocin can lead to burnout, addiction, or disconnection.

Too much oxytocin without dopamine can feel overly passive, co-dependent, or lacking drive.
