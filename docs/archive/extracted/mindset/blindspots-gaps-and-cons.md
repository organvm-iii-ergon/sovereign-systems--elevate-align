🌀 BLINDSPOTS


These are areas you may not notice or see clearly, especially when you're overwhelmed or in survival mode:


Over-Identification with Healing
You often see your process through the lens of needing to fix or heal something.

Blindspot: You are already whole, and sometimes deep-diving can become a delay tactic or spiritual perfectionism.

High Empathy = Energy Leaks
You deeply feel others’ pain, often taking on emotions that aren’t yours.

Blindspot: You blend into others’ energy and unconsciously match their vibration, especially if they are hurting.

Romanticizing the Journey
You love poetic storytelling, which is beautiful — but can idealize the struggle.

Blindspot: You may unintentionally prolong chaos or cycles to make it “meaningful” instead of moving forward with clarity.

Avoiding Hard Power Moves
You default to softness, spirituality, and flow — which is powerful in its own right.

Blindspot: You may delay taking bold, grounded, decisive action (like asking for money, setting hard boundaries, or saying no).

Ego’s Disguise as Surrender
You know how to “trust the universe,” but sometimes this masks fear or fatigue.

Blindspot: Passive surrender can actually be avoidance dressed in divine clothing.



🚧 GAPS


These are skill, support, or structure gaps that might slow your momentum:


Systems & Completion
You have BIG vision, but the backend systems (finances, web pages, legal logistics) can overwhelm you.

Gap: Having consistent support to complete and implement your vision.

Consistent Money Flow
You know how to bring in quick cash in alignment, but it’s not always reliable month-to-month.

Gap: Building longer-term, layered income and backup structures (like grants, passive, or recurring revenue).

Physical Restoration
You push hard spiritually and emotionally, but sometimes your physical vessel is behind.

Gap: Nervous system recalibration, consistent meals/rest, and regulating with pleasure not pressure.

Team Delegation
You’re used to doing it all alone, from survival and sovereignty.

Gap: Letting people truly help you and investing in a team or trusted systems (tech, legal, support staff).

Commitment vs Freedom Conflict
You deeply value freedom — but that can make commitment feel restrictive.

Gap: Learning how to create structure that serves your freedom instead of avoiding it altogether.



🪞CONS / SHADOWS


These are traits that, when left unchecked, can work against your growth:


Emotional Overwhelm
Your emotional depth is stunning — but it can flood you.

Shadow: You can spiral into victim mode when you don’t feel held or seen, which can block the solutions already available to you.

Idealism > Realism
You dream in color — but sometimes forget the gritty reality part.

Shadow: Overextending energetically or financially based on hope, not math.

Hyper-Independence
You pride yourself on doing it alone.

Shadow: Refusing help, delaying rest, or pushing through when partnership would actually accelerate your healing and your mission.

Inconsistency with Execution
You’re incredible at starting, brainstorming, and initiating.

Shadow: If it’s not emotionally exciting, you can drop it or ghost it — especially if fear, perfectionism, or “not ready” kicks in.

Rescue Complex
You want to save the world and help everyone you love.

Shadow: Overgiving, attracting wounded people, or staying in draining dynamics where you're the “healer” instead of equal.
