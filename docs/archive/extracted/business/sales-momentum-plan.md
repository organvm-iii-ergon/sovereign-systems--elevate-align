## Response:
That makes total sense — and you’re actually right on track.
You’re not blocked — you’re just in the “bridge” phase between setup and activation. This phase is all about getting the foundation airtight so you can launch confidently instead of chaotically. Let’s get you a simple, clear roadmap so you know exactly what to do next.



🌊 PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Today–Tomorrow)


Goal: Finish the back-end setup so when you start posting or messaging, everything connects cleanly.


Checklist:


GHL Setup
✅ Finish “High Level Setup Doc” and “Business Launch Course” (just skim and do only what’s essential)

✅ Make sure your calendar + email automations are linked

✅ Confirm that name/email fields + tags are set correctly for the funnel

Water Funnel Setup
Upload your landing page video (teaser or full)

Add your logo, branding, and “Submit before continuing” note

Link your purchase page + thank-you page

Test the full flow (submit > email > calendar > thank-you)

Biz Hub / Resource Hub
Add: “Start Here,” “FAQ,” “Discovery Process,” “Next Steps”

Keep it super visual and intuitive — think of it as the HQ for everyone you enroll



💬 PHASE 2: ACTIVATION (Next 2–3 Days)


Goal: Build trust + visibility through story-driven posts and personal messages.
Start with soft activation — showing what you’re doing, not pitching yet.


Structure:


Day 1: Post a story: “Rebuilding everything behind the scenes — I’m so close to relaunching this water project and it feels surreal.”

Day 2: Post a Reel: your face + caption like “Water literally changed my life. This time, I’m doing it right — systems, flow, freedom.”

Day 3: Start outreach — 5–10 warm DMs using something like:“Hey [Name]! You popped into my mind when I was finishing my water hub today. Are you still curious about the health side or business side of the water?”



🔁 PHASE 3: FOLLOW-UP FLOW


Goal: Keep momentum and move people from interest → connection → call.


Follow-Up Template:


Day 0: Send initial message or post CTA

Day 2:“Hey, just circling back — I had a few people hop on a call this week who said they’d been watching for months. Don’t want you to miss out if you’re still interested.”

Day 5:“I get it — timing is everything. Just wanted to leave this here: [your funnel link]. You can watch the webclass anytime!”

Day 7+: Add them to your nurture list (stories, value posts, client results)



🔥 Optional Add-Ons


I can help you:
Write 1 clean funnel script flow (headline → offer → CTA)

Build your 3-day activation content lineup

Create a DM follow-up tracker template to stay consistent


Would you like me to start with the DM follow-up tracker (so you can see your flow and who to message each day) or the 3-day activation content lineup first?
