## Summary
This document treats Maddie as more than a client record or site backlog. It
asks a harder question: what evidence exists that Maddie is already present in
the system as person, doctrine, brand, corpus, implementation, operation, and
test case?

The answer is uneven but substantial.

Maddie exists strongly as a dense symbolic and documentary formation. She also
exists as a partially implemented business instrument: a live repository, a
deployed site, a structured intake, a named revenue logic, and a set of open
operational dependencies. She does **not** yet exist as closed-loop public
proof. The lane has high density and low closure.

## The Missing Mutuality
At present, Maddie and the system are asymmetrically related.

`[Evidence]` Maddie has already given the system a large amount of source
material, reaction, symbolic direction, and commercial pressure.

`[Evidence]` The system has given back architecture, implementation, issue
tracking, transmutation, and partial deployment.

`[Inference]` What is still missing is mutual closure: the point where Maddie
becomes not only a source of material and a target of work, but a validated
node in the larger system's proof structure.

1. `content existence`

3. `personal existence`

`[Inference]` Maddie is already real as worldview, corpus, and collaborator.
