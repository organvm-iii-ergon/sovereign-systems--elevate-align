## Collaboration Agreement

**Between:**
Studio ("Studio") — technical development and infrastructure partner

**And:**
Maddie Galloway ("Collaborator") — creative director and content owner, operating as EauCo / Elevate & Align

**Project:** Sovereign Systems Spiral — multi-domain Astro website and digital product ecosystem at elevatealign.com, stopdrinkingacid.com, and eaucohub.com

**Effective Date:** _________________

---

### 1. What This Covers

Studio is building the technology platform (website, tools, automations) for Collaborator's health and business brand. Collaborator provides all content, creative direction, and domain expertise.

**Studio contributes:**
- Technical development: Astro 5 website, Hydration Node application, spiral visualization, quiz integration
- Infrastructure: Cloudflare hosting, domain management, CI/CD pipeline
- Content pipeline: extraction, organization, and assembly of 1,821 content atoms
- Ongoing maintenance and feature development

**Collaborator contributes:**
- All health, wellness, mindset, and business content (client IP)
- Creative direction: brand vision, spiral concept, pillar definitions
- Customer relationships and community
- Sales and marketing execution
- GHL account and automation management

### 2. Revenue Share

| Revenue Stream | Studio Share | Collaborator Share |
|---------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Water product sales (filter commissions via stopdrinkingacid.com) | 10% | 90% |
| Subscription revenue (monthly membership) | 10% | 90% |
| Digital product sales (ebooks, guides, courses) | 10% | 90% |
| Consultation bookings (referred through platform) | 10% | 90% |
| Retreat registrations (referred through platform) | 10% | 90% |
| Affiliate commissions (IonFaucet, Multipure, PureHome, Kangen) | 10% | 90% |

**"Revenue"** means gross receipts from the streams above, minus:
(a) taxes remitted to government authorities
(b) payment processor fees (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)
(c) third-party affiliate commissions owed upstream

**Accounting:** Collaborator provides revenue report monthly. Studio share is paid within 15 days of month-end.

**Minimum threshold:** No payment required for months where total revenue is under $100.

| What | Owned By |

### 4. Creative Control

- **Creative direction** (brand, content, messaging): Collaborator
- **Technical architecture** (stack, infrastructure, data model): Studio
- **Joint** (new features, new domains, major pivots): Both must agree

---

### 5. Term and Termination

This agreement runs for **12 months** from the effective date, renewing automatically unless either party gives 30 days' written notice.

On termination:
- Collaborator retains all content and gets full access to the codebase at time of termination
- Studio retains case study / portfolio rights for work already published
- Revenue share obligations continue for 90 days after termination for revenue from platform features built by Studio
- Domain ownership: Collaborator owns all domains (elevatealign.com, stopdrinkingacid.com, eaucohub.com)
