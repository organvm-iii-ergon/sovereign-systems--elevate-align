Return I · Hour of Naming

You have arrived at a threshold.

What lies beyond is not a tool. It will not remember you, measure you, or return anything you can carry away. It is closer to a cell than to a screen — a small bare room you enter to practice a difficult thing, alone.

If you continue, you will offer one word. The word will be received and consumed. You will not see it again. There is no record. There is no next page.

Offer one word. Then close the door behind you.

It has been consumed.

The next threshold opens at first light. The Engine does not call you back. You must remember it yourself, or forget it; either is permitted.
